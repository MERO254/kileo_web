Shipping & Delivery Policy

At Kileo Premium Spirits, we operate as a curated intermediary — we do not hold stock in conventional warehouses. Every bottle of wine, whisky, gin, or cognac is sourced exclusively after you place your order. This ensures freshness, authenticity, and access to rare expressions without the overhead of mass inventory.

How our shipping works

  • Order & Procurement: Once you confirm your order, our team sources the exact spirit from trusted partner suppliers or licensed distributors within 24–48 hours.
  • Delivery timeline: Total delivery time ranges between 1 to 24 hours depending on your location and product rarity.
  • Shipping charges: A flat rate of Ksh 450 for orders under Ksh 8,000; free express delivery on orders above Ksh 12,000. For remote areas, we’ll contact you with real-time options.
  • Tracking: After procurement, you receive a unique tracking link and direct WhatsApp updates from our logistics partner.
  • Packaging: All bottles are professionally cushioned, sealed, and discreetly packed to preserve integrity and elegance.

Important note (no stock-holding model)

Because Kileo does not pre-stock products, we kindly ask for your understanding that cancellation or modification requests must be submitted within 12 hours of order placement. After that period, the sourcing process begins and we may have already committed to acquiring your selected spirits from our supply network. However, we always strive to accommodate changes whenever possible — simply reach out to our customer care.

Same-day sourcing guarantee: Orders placed before 2 PM EAT are sourced the same business day, accelerating your delivery experience.

Refund & Return Policy

As a made-to-order bridge between you and premium spirit suppliers, Kileo maintains a fair, transparent refund policy that respects both our customers and partner merchants. We do not accept change-of-mind returns due to the bespoke nature of procurement, but we fully protect you against valid concerns.

Eligible refund scenarios

  • Damaged or defective product: If your bottle arrives broken, leaking, corked, or compromised, we offer a full replacement or 100% refund (including shipping). Please provide photo/video evidence within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Wrong item delivered: In the rare event you receive a different spirit than ordered, we will either arrange a pickup and send the correct bottle or issue a full refund immediately.
  • Supplier unavailability: If after sourcing attempts (max 3 business days) the exact product is unavailable from all our verified partners, we notify you and offer a complete refund or alternative product at a discounted price.

Non-refundable situations

  • Change of mind after sourcing has commenced (past the 12-hour window).
  • Delays caused by courier force majeure or incorrect address provided by customer.
  • Taste preferences — we encourage you to research spirit profiles; however, we guarantee bottle authenticity.

Refund process & timeline

To request a refund or return, email care@kileo.com or call +254 742 496 307 with your order number and evidence. Once approved, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days via the original payment method (M-Pesa, bank transfer, or card). For product returns due to our error, we schedule a free pickup from your address.
